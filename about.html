About the Founder

Anna Lukasiak is the founder of Data Gallery, a project management consultancy specializing in software and technology initiatives. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Engineering and a Master's degree in Economics from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).

With over 10 years of experience working on Wall Street, Anna brings a strategic and analytical approach to delivering complex, high-impact projects. She is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP), Certified ScrumMaster (CSM), and SAFe Program Consultant (SPC), and has successfully led initiatives across a wide range of industries including financial services, healthcare, agriculture, automotive, social media, entertainment, and apparel.

Anna has worked with enterprise, mid-size, and small organizations across the private, public, and non-profit sectors, helping them translate technology visions into executable, value-driven solutions. She was a featured speaker at the PMI Global Summit,, where she presented on navigating the challenges of AI project implementation—a reflection of her commitment to guiding organizations through the evolving landscape of digital transformation.
